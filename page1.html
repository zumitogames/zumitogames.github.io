About Us

We are David López, programmer, and Daniel Piqueras, designer — two partners united by a passion for creating unique and different games. Our mission is to take “juicy” gameplay to the next level by developing small games that deliver value and memorable experiences. With a distinctive pixel art style, we strive to make each project stand out through originality and charm, offering players fun and lively moments.
